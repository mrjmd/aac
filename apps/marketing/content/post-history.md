# Post History Manifest

Recent posts published across all platforms. Used as context during idea
generation to avoid topic duplication and ensure template variety.

Entries are newest-first. Each entry should have enough detail for the AI to
recognize if a new idea is too similar to something already posted.

## Format

```
### YYYY-MM-DD — Short Topic Title
- **Platforms:** Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, GBP
- **Pillar:** Educational | Showcase | Seasonal | Testimonial | Personality | Blog
- **Template:** A | C | D | F | G | H (or "video", "photo-only")
- **Topic:** One-sentence summary of the post's main message
- **Key phrases:** Notable phrases used (for dedup matching)
```
